Analyzing Global Terrorism Trends

According to Merriam-Webster, terrorism is the "unlawful use or threat of violence especially against the state or the public as a politically motivated means of attack or coercion." However, there is no universally agreed definition making it difficult to explain and quantify.

It is without a doubt that this is not a 21st century phenomenon as its roots came from early religious groups trying to overcome one another. Modern day terrorism came largely into play after World War II with the culmination of political/religous groups worldwide to influence and transform people into their own beliefs. It was an effective way of paving local conflicts into international issues.
